The purpose behind Greenergy

The purpose behind Greenergy was to create a website that gave homeowners the ability to start thinking about their energy and practices on manage to keep green, clean energy. This website was built for the TSA Webmaster competition event.

Why provide this information?

The purpose of providing this information is to give a way homeowners can switch to green, clean energy. This website is very informational with how homeowners can switch.

What is TSA?

TSA (Technology Student Association) is a STEM extraciricular students can participate in by allowing them to compete in different STEM activities. TSA can expand ones mind by showing what people can accomplish in the STEM field. You can learn more about TSA at the official website: tsaweb.org
